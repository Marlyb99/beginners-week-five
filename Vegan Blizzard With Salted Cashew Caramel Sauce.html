Vegan Blizzard With Salted Cashew Caramel Sauce

INGREDIENTS

FOR THE SALTED CASHEW-CARAMEL SAUCE:

            
  • 1/2 cup Organics® organic refined coconut oil
    •         
  • 4 tsp Organics® raw blue agave syrup
    •         
  • 3 TB Open Nature® creamy cashew butter
    •         
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • a generous pinch or two of flaky sea salt
    •     

FOR THE VEGAN BLIZZARD

            
  • 1 container Open Nature® Salted Caramel Cluster Cashewmilk Non-Dairy Frozen Dessert, left out to slightly defrost for 5–10 minutes
    •         
  • 1/3 cup Organics® unsweetened, organic original almondmilk
    •         
  • handful of Organics® organic nut trio blend, crushed in a food processor
    •         
  • handful of dark chocolate vegan chunks
    •     

Instructions

FOR THE SALTED CASHEW-CARAMEL SAUCE
In a small saucepan, melt coconut oil over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in agave and cashew butter. Adjust agave and salt to taste, if desired. Stir until smooth. Add in vanilla and salt. Transfer caramel mixture to a bowl to cool at room temp. Caramel with darken and thicken as it cools after about an hour. Drizzle over ice cream!

FOR THE VEGAN BLIZZARD
IPlace the empty bowl of a KitchenAid into the freezer for at least 30 minutes to chill. Transfer cashewmilk ice cream and almondmilk into bowl. Using the paddle attachment on low, beat for 15 seconds until slightly smooth. Do not overmix. Transfer to bowls or glasses. Top with the salted caramel, nuts and vegan chocolate chunks.
